Our Story

Where Every Recipe Tells a Story

Mannat Bakery was born from a simple dream — to fill every home with the warmth of freshly baked goods. Founded in 2021 by our head baker, each creation is crafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the finest organic ingredients sourced locally.

From rustic sourdough loaves to elaborate wedding cakes, we pour our heart into every single batch. Mannat means "a heartfelt wish" — and that is exactly what we bake into every creation.
